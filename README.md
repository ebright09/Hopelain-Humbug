## ğŸ® The Game

This isn't a quiz; it's a **strategic audit**. Navigate through 10 sessions of marketing intensity, from Value Creation to The Modern CMO Mindset.

### Rules of Engagement
- **7/10 to pass** each bank
- **3 strikes = WASTED** (career liquidation)
- **10/10 = IPO** (strategic ascension)
- **300 MBA-level questions** across 10 sessions

### Sessions
1. **Value Creation** - The Wedge between survival and domination
2. **Brand Audits** - Architecture for the architecturally gifted
3. **Brand Soul** - Liquid Death & the art of selling water to goths
4. **Positioning** - Climbing the ladder without falling off
5. **Influencers** - Trust transfer & the leasing of souls
6. **Content Strategy** - Away's playbook for category domination
7. **Performance** - Funnels, metrics & the art of conversion
8. **Loyalty** - Byron Sharp vs. everyone else
9. **Brand Moats** - Building fortresses for fun and profit
10. **Modern CMO** - From creative director to P&L owner
